About Me

Get to know more about my background and expertise

I am a highly skilled and results-driven Backend Developer with over 5 years of experience in designing and optimizing robust backend systems. My expertise lies in developing scalable RESTful APIs and gRPC services, integrating complex third-party platforms, and enhancing system performance using modern frameworks and best practices.

Beyond implementation, I have strong capabilities in mapping application architecture from start to finish, including crafting Entity Relationship Diagrams (ERD), Data Flow Diagrams (DFD), and database schemas tailored for both small-scale and enterprise-level systems. I'm also experienced in designing efficient table maps and minimizing query load through indexing, caching strategies, and optimized query structures.

With a strong analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities, I excel in collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-performing, maintainable, and scalable systems. I am a fast learner, highly adaptable to new technologies, and thrive under pressure.
